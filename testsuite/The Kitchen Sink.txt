The Kitchen Sink

5

Spices 1500 tbsp salt
Canned 12 cans sardines
Produce 8 bell peppers
Produce 3 heads cabbage
Produce 3 carrots
Produce 2.5 yellow onions
Dairy 1 scoop ice cream

Directions

1. Take the actual sink in your kitchen and rip it out.
2. Throw all ingredients into the gaping hole left after ripping your sink out
3. Mix it up real good
4. Eat with a (small) spoon
